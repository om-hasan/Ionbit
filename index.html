Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Find quick answers to common questions about our services, process, and how we can help bring your digital vision to life.

What services does IONBIT offer?

We specialize in custom software development, visual communication design, branding solutions, digital platforms, and comprehensive media production. Our creative approach ensures every project delivers both stunning aesthetics and powerful functionality.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. Simple branding projects may take 2-3 weeks, while custom software development can range from 6-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation phase.

Do you work with small businesses or only large corporations?

We work with businesses of all sizes! From startups needing their first brand identity to established corporations requiring complex digital platforms, we tailor our approach to fit your budget and goals.

What's included in your design systems service?

Our design systems include comprehensive brand guidelines, color palettes, typography standards, UI components, icon libraries, and implementation documentation to ensure consistency across all your digital touchpoints.

Can you help improve our existing website's performance?

Absolutely! We offer website optimization services including performance audits, user experience improvements, mobile responsiveness upgrades, and conversion rate optimization to boost your digital presence.

How do you ensure projects stay within budget?

We provide detailed project estimates upfront and maintain transparent communication throughout. Our agile approach allows for regular check-ins and adjustments to keep projects on track and within agreed parameters.
